# Hapax Legomenon

- a term that occurs only once within a text
- related terms
  - dis legomenon
  - tris legomenon
  - tetrakis legomenon
- are quite common (For large corpora, about 40% to 60% of the words are hapax legomena, and another 10% to 15% are dis legomena)
